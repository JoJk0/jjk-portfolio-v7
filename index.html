About me

My name is Jakub Janisz. I am 20 years old and I am keen webdesigner and webdeveloper. I am from Poland and live in Liverpool, England and study Computer Science at The University of Liverpool. I develop my programming skills since childhood. At junior high school I was a finalist of regional competition. Later, I took part in Google Code-In 2012. Since September I will go to university in UK. Every project I make is always with love and care. My aim is to develop my skills instead of earn money. In my free time I am riding a bike so much and also travelling to inspiring places.

My workspace

I always attach importance to appearance of place where I am working. I believe that it influences on creativity, adds more energy and makes you more productive. I love every kind of minimalism - from interior design to websites. Well-organised, clutter-free workspace is an essential to work efficiently.
